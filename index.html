Projects

Missed Load Calculator

As a startup company, I developed a C# program to read .csv files with glass furnace production data. The program determines optimal processing times based on historical data and highlights delayed loads, showing missed load information and counts. I added a configurable delay feature to exclude minor delays from being categorized as missed. Later, I integrated a total timer to track cumulative lost time due to missed loads, aiding process optimization. Originally a temporary solution, the program significantly improved production efficiency.

Machine Learning - Audio Classification

This dissertation focuses on the potential security risks associated with acoustic keystroke identification in authentication processes. By assembling diverse datasets from various audio recording devices and typing styles, including the utilization of a blue-switch mechanical keyboard, the study assesses the adaptability and reliability of acoustic keystroke identification under real-life working conditions. Achieving a high average classification accuracy of 88.61%, the research employs various machine learning algorithms and frequency extraction techniques.
